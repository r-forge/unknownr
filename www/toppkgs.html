Crantastic's ranking seems inappropriate for unknownR's needs:
  • Packages with just one 5* vote (and no other votes) are ranked first because they have the maximum vote of 5.000.
  • ggplot2 is ranked 41st (when writing this), which doesn't seem correct given it has the most votes and the most users, by far.
  • Some packages on page 2 and 3 have many votes and users, but are harder to find.
The data is scraped and a default 3* vote is added to each package to address these issues.
No claim is made that this is the best method, just that it is better than Crantastic's rank.
